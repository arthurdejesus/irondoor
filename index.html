The Key To Success

A trusted process

Step01Measurements

Provide rough measurents, CAD drawings, Blueprints or have us measure onsite. (your rough measurements are close enough until we get to the CAD drawing) (we can also pull the measurements off your house plans for new construction) (remodels... we can measure your house if in the Dallas, Fort Worth, Burleson, or Tulsa area)

Step02Design

We collect your wish list and design ideas.

Step03Top of Door

Decide on square top, arch top, or eyebrow top.

Step04Transom windows option

Decide on Transom windows. (windows attached directly above the door)

Step05Sidelights option

Decide on Sidelights. (windows attached directly to the sides of the door)

Step06Select a Color

Decide on a door color - see samples here

Step07Door Swing

Decide on LH or RH Swing. (this is the side the hinges are on, not the handle, if looking at the outside of the door)

Step08Choose a Glass Texture

Options include Clear, Rain Opbscured, Water Cubic Obscured, Clear

Step09Choose a Lock set

Decide on using a standard 2 hole lock set or a 1 hole with catch roller system. (catch roller system consist of a ornamental iron handle set and a standard dead bolt lock) (the catch roller system holds the door shut when not locked)
